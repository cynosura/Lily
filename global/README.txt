About the directory 'global'
----------------------------

This directory contains projects which are not specific to Lily. That is,
they could be reused outside of Lily, or they could even have existed
outside of Lily.

Besides some bigger projects like the rowlog and hbaseindex, this also
contains utility projects. These are not projects which we imagine to be
reused outside of Lily, but they are here because they are reused by
both the content repository as well as the applications.
